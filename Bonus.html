Step by step guide to making a sandwich

  1. get 2 slices of bread or slice a loaf or roll in half
  2. spread your condiments of choice, like mayonnaise or mustard, on the inside or cut side of the bread
  3. Layer your fillings on top of the first slice of bread
  4. place the second slice on top, with the condiment side down.
